A MIND SANG
A MÃE DE SANGUE

a short film about perception, rebirth and transformation

Play Trailer
Close

SYNOPSIS

A pregnant scientist becomes a mother, a queer couple draws and a cat observes butterflies.
Their stories merge to form a dreamlike celebration of rebirth and transformation.

A short by VIER NEV

DIRECTION & ANIMATION

PreviousNextClose

Every frame an image with two possible interpretations - A Mind Sang / A Mãe de Sangue is the result of a weird visual study on the limits of simultaneous storytelling. I have always wanted to make a film that celebrated the joy of drawing and playing with perception.

Vier Nev
