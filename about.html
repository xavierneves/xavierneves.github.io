WHO?



Vier Nev is a Portuguese multidisciplinary artist.

Vier is interested in creating experiences that challenge the viewers’ perception and explore the artistic potential of emerging technologies, with a particular interest in Virtual Reality.

_

Vier studied Multimedia at Escola Superior de Artes e Design (ESMAD) and is currently studying for a Masters Degree in Virtual Reality at London College of Communication, University of the Arts London.  They have developed skills in AR, VR, 2D animation, graphic design, UI/UX design, 3D modeling and animation, editing, post-production, SFX, motion graphics and art direction.
Vier's latest projects include a short film, an AR exhibition and a VR experience.
